About Me

I'm Steve Xander Odera, a Nairobi-based Product Designer who's passionate about crafting meaningful digital solutions that are inclusive, accessible, and user-first. With a strong foundation in UX/UI design, I specialize in turning complex problems into simple, elegant user experiences.

My work focuses on impactful design rooted in strategy and empathy. Whether it's improving communication in a ride-hailing app or evaluating accessibility in a global streaming platform, I design with people at the center.

I'm driven by curiosity, collaboration, and the desire to build products that make a difference.
